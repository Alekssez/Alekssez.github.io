Frequently Asked Questions

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

At http://print.hz.nl you log in with your HZ account and uplaod the file that you want to print.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

At a HZ printer you can scan your pass, choose scan and the files are send to your mail box.

Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?

Contact the teachers/helpdesk at school and stay home. Don't forget to follow the lessons on stream.

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

On the home page on Self Service Portal you can choose Resevations % visitors and you will be able to book a room.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Activate your HZ pass at first floor and when you want to park you just scan your pass.
